What is PP?

Pair programming is a strategy for developers to approach a hands-on subject on one computer. Usually, one person drives while the other navigates and the pair shares and discuss ideas as they work together. Pair programming can be done either in person or online. A key benefit is the ability to work out problems with another person and have another set of eyes to check your work.

Why is PP important to DBC

Pair programming is important to learning, People with different learning style are working together. Most of us are beginners. You can learn a lot by pairing with someone who is better than you. If you know thing that you can teach your pair, it can solidfy that topic by explaining or teaching that concept to your peer. Everyone is supporting eachother and everyone is working problems out together.

Compare Driver and Navigator

Both throw out ideas and help eachother. The driver will be the one pair of hands on a computer at a time, so that person will get hands on experience. The other with get experience getting eyes on the project and will be able to look up references and jot down notes while the driver codes.
